03 · The Person About · CV

A long road, still walking.

Hi, I'm Emeka — a Creative Director and 3D generalist with nineteen years of creative battle scars to show off. I count myself humbled to have worked on several ground-breaking campaigns ranging from print and digital to experiential.

I currently lead creative at DDB Lagos. Before that, I led teams at Tequila Nigeria, TCC (Insight Publicis Groupe), and Synthesis — building campaigns for Hennessy, Campari, Tiger Beer, Renault, Pepsi, FirstBank, MRS Oil, 7Up, Cadbury, Heineken, Google, YouTube, Visa, and many more.

Working in the fast-paced, ever-changing advertising environment requires one to stay abreast of design trends — and over time, I've acquired the skills necessary to stay ahead of the curve. I'm an expert user of Adobe design tools like Photoshop, Illustrator, and XD, as well as 3D design software like Cinema 4D and Blender. The tools are the easy part. The harder, more enduring craft is knowing what is worth making in the first place.
